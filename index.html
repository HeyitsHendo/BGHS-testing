Frequently Asked Questions

Do you guarantee to remove all stains?

Some products can permanently damage the carpet fibres which makes it impossible to give a guarantee.

How long will my carpet take to dry?

All carpet types take a varying amount of time to dry depending on the thickness of the carpet, the temperature and airflow and humidity in the house.

How long will my lounge take to dry?

A majority of lounge fabrics will take between 4 to 5 hours to dry depending on the temperature, airflow and humidity in the house.
