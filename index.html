Do you guarantee to remove all stains from carpet and upholstery?

Some products can permanently stain the carpet fibres which makes it impossible to give a guarantee.

How long will my carpet take to dry?

All carpet types take a varying amount of time to dry depending on the thickness of the carpet, the temperature in the house and humidity in the house.

How long will my lounge take to dry?

A majority of lounge fabrics will take between 2 to 4 hours to dry depending on the temperature and humidity in the house.
