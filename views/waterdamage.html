Water Damage Restoration

Flood damage or burst pipes can cause extensive harm to furniture and all types of flooring, including underlay and subfloors. Walls often need thorough inspection, as mold can become a serious issue if not addressed by experienced professionals. Simply removing excess water is insufficient to resolve problems caused by burst pipes or floods.

Our team uses specialized equipment to prevent environments conducive to mould growth. With over 20 years of experience in water damage restoration, we provide comprehensive plans to restore areas damaged by everything from floods to accidental spills in homes and offices.

Our technicians work closely with insurance companies to ensure a smooth and efficient restoration experience.

Trust Blue Gum Home Services for expert water damage restoration services that prioritize your safety and satisfaction.
