Carpet & Upholstery

At Blue Gum Home Services, we offer professional carpet and upholstery cleaning services tailored to meet your needs. Our advanced machinery and specialized chemical process ensures your carpets and upholstery are the cleanest and most hygienic in the Hunter region.

Our state-of-the-art equipment reaches deep into your carpets, extracting dirt and grime that regular cleaning methods miss. This ensures your carpets not only look their best but are thoroughly sanitized. As a locally owned business, we proudly source all our products from local suppliers, supporting our community and guaranteeing quality.

Our experienced technicians are adept at handling even the most challenging fabrics, ensuring your lounges and upholstery are clean, fresh, and dry within approximately 6 hours*. Our powerful cleaning system removes grease, body oils, and embedded dirt without leaving any chemical residue.

How Do We Do It?

Experience the difference with us for a cleaner, healthier, and more professional environment.

Step by Step

  1. Vacuum carpet thoroughly
  2. Treat the stains with our spot solution
  3. Combine hot water & carpet solution into the extraction machine
  4. Thoroughly clean the carpet with the hot water extraction machine
  5. Drypass the carpet to enhance drying time
